My Homwtown


The name of my home town is DHAKA The capital of Bangladesh. It is the country's largest and most important city. known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and rapid urbanization.

Dhaka is a place where tradition meets modernity. From the historic landmarks of Lalbagh Fort and Ahsan Manzil to the towering skyscrapers and shopping malls, the city is always alive with energy. It is famous for its delicious street food, the colorful celebrations of Pohela Boishakh, and its countless rickshaws navigating through busy roads. Despite challenges like traffic congestion and overpopulation, Dhaka continues to grow and evolve, with new developments such as the metro rail and smart city projects shaping its future. For me, Dhaka is more than just a city—it is home, filled with memories, warmth, and the spirit of resilience.

With a population exceeding 20 million, it is one of the most densely populated cities in the world. As the country’s economic, political, and cultural center, Dhaka is home to major industries, including textiles, finance, and technology. Holds several titles and nicknames, including:
city of Mosqe – Due to its numerous historic and modern mosques.

Rickshaw Capital of the World – As it has one of the highest numbers of cycle rickshaws in the world.

Megacity Dhaka– As one of the most densely populated megacities globally.

Heart of Bangladesh – Being the political, economic, and cultural center of the country.
